Frequently Asked Questions

- Here are some common inquiries -

Are you looking a new full time role?

As a freelance developer, I realize how much freedom I have with my career and can control the direction it is going. With that being said, I know I still have a lot to learn and am open to opportunity!

How many years of experience do you have?

I have only been studying in this career field for 1 year, and only recently taking on projects. I do this full-time now and I am excited to have the opportunity to help everyone create their dream website, whether it be for their aspiring business or their own personal blog. The options are endless! 

Are you interested in international roles?

I love to travel and see the world, and living in another country has always been open to me. Location is definitely a heavy deciding factor, but as I said above, I am open to the potential opportunity!

What are your consulting rates?

Initial consultations are completely free! I am willing to set out up to a 30-minute block of my day to make sure that everything is covered, see what direction we are heading with the potential project, and to really get to know what the project means to you. From then on, we can discuss what applicable fees there are and how we can proceed!
