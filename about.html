/ My Story

My first website design in 2021

Whenever I first got into coding, I thought that it was going to take ages before I could ever code a website. The more I learned, the more everything starting piecing together. After only a couple weeks, I was able to create a nice demo landing page that looked exactly like a page off of the website of Adidas!
After that, I started specializing in learning about Shopify and e-Commerce stores. After months of work, I was able to finally quit my full-time job as a medic and become a freelance developer!

/ My Story

How I started as a web developer

Growing up, I always knew I wanted to be in the tech field, but I first went with becoming a medic through the Air National Guard because all of my family was in the medical field. Once I came to terms that I didn't enjoy the profession, I found a company called Freemote. Thanks to Aaron Jack, Jan Frey, and the rest of the crew over at Freemote, they guided me to a profession that I have come to love and enjoy!
